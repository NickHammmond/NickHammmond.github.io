Our final Project


Hammond_Final

Our final project was to create a game based on what we learned throughout the semester.
Our project is neon air hockey that is two players, one player controls via the mouse the other controls by using the arrow keys.
The two different bumpers or paddles taht yoy cab control both can contact the puck and the objective is to get the puck into the goal.
Moving the bottom cursor with the mouse and top cursor with the arrow keys, the puck can change direction based on how you hit it.
When the puck crosses the goal it is reset the middle where the players will then fight for control of it again.
